BIMP

Bytom Improvement Proposals (BMIPs) describe standards for the Bytom platform, including core protocol, client APIs, VM specifications and contract standards. Having a BMIP here does not make it a formally accepted standard until its status becomes Final or Active.
Those proposing changes should consider that ultimately consent may rest with the consensus of the Bytom users.
Review BMIP-001 and follow the instructions to submit a Pull Request to this repository.
